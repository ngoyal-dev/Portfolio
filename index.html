Tab 1
Executive Dashboard
The Executive Dashboard is the single-screen portfolio story for board meetings. It surfaces total portfolio rNPV, 10-year cumulative revenue, R&D investment, and pipeline-to-inline mix — all at a glance. Below the KPIs, you see a financial projection chart (revenue, gross margin, operating profit), a TA performance table with ROI ratios, upcoming inflection points, and auto-generated strategic alerts flagging LOE cliffs and concentration risks.
💰
KPI Summary Cards
rNPV, 10Y revenue, R&D spend, pipeline/inline mix — each with an "Ask AI" sparkle trigger
📈
Financial Projections
Revenue, gross margin, and operating profit visualized over the 10-year window
⚠️
Strategic Alerts
Auto-generated flags: LOE cliffs, TA concentration, pipeline gaps — click "Ask AI" to prioritize
🎯
TA Performance Table
Revenue, rNPV, investment, and ROI ratio per therapeutic area for portfolio balance review
Example Use Case
"I have 10 minutes before the board meeting — show me the portfolio story, top risks, and what's changed since last quarter."
📉
Tab 2
Revenue Forecast / LRP
The Revenue Forecast tab is the long-range planning engine. A 10-year stacked area chart lets you toggle between three views: by Therapeutic Area, Inline vs. Pipeline, and PTRS Risk Buckets. Below the chart, customizable strategic periods (Investment Phase, Growth Phase, LOE Impact) show revenue, pipeline contribution, and growth rates for each window. A dedicated Pipeline Potential view compares risk-adjusted revenue against the full unrisked (100% PTRS) upside for every pipeline program.
🔀
3-Way Toggle
Switch between TA view, Inline vs Pipeline, and PTRS risk buckets with one click
📅

Investment, Growth, and LOE Impact phases with revenue totals and growth rates
🏆

Top 5 programs ranked by cumulative 10Y revenue contribution
🔓
Pipeline Upside
Risk-adjusted vs 100% PTRS comparison reveals how much value is locked behind clinical risk
Example Use Case
"What percentage of our 2030 revenue depends on programs with less than 50% PTRS? Show me where the risk-adjusted vs. unrisked gap is largest."

Tab 3
R&D Portfolio Review
The R&D Portfolio Review has three sub-tabs. The Portfolio Summary shows a visual map of every program by TA and development phase, with a timeline of upcoming readouts, launches, and LOEs. The R&D Budget sub-tab breaks down committed vs. future spend by TA and stage, including PTRS-weighted expected spend. The Valuation sub-tab is where the strategic magic happens: a risk-reward bubble chart with quadrant analysis, value unlock rankings, and a full program scorecard — each row with an AI sparkle trigger for instant deep dives.
🗺️
Portfolio Map
TA × Phase matrix — click any program pill to ask the AI about it instantly
💸
Budget Analysis
Committed spend, total future spend, and PTRS-weighted expected future spend
🫧
Risk-Reward Scatter
PTRS vs rNPV bubble chart with quadrants: Protect, Calculated Bets, Manage, Kill Zone
⬆️
Value Unlock
Incremental rNPV gained if each program's current phase succeeds — ranked visually
Example Use Case
"Are we over-investing in Neuroscience relative to rNPV? Which programs sit in the 'kill zone' and should we cut them?"

Tab 4
Program Deep Dive
This is the 360-degree view for go/no-go decisions. Select any program from the dropdown and instantly see its complete profile: molecule, TA, indication, PTRS, all spend figures, launch and LOE timelines. The revenue projection chart overlays risk-adjusted revenue against the 100% PTRS potential with an LOE reference line. Most critically, the Portfolio Impact panel shows what happens if this program advances — how it changes revenue contribution, growth trajectory, and committed spend. This is the view designed for CMOs and CFOs at portfolio committee meetings.

Tab 5
Portfolio Optimization
The Optimization tab is where strategy meets math. Select an objective — maximize rNPV, optimize cash flow, maximize value unlock, or balance risk-adjusted returns. Manually include or exclude programs before running scenarios. The ranked output shows which programs make the cut, and the Efficiency Frontier visualization shows the maximum achievable portfolio value at each budget constraint level — revealing whether you're leaving value on the table and where reallocation would move you closer to the optimal curve.
🎛️
4 Optimization Objectives

Tab 6
AI Analyst & Co-Pilot
The AI isn't just a tab — it's woven into every surface. A persistent slide-out co-pilot panel is accessible from any tab via the floating chat button. It knows which view you're on, which program you've selected, and surfaces contextual suggested questions. On metric cards, chart headers, and scorecard rows, small sparkle triggers open the panel with pre-loaded questions. The AI generates interactive charts inline — bar, line, pie, scatter, and tables — right within the conversation. The standalone AI Analyst tab offers a full-page experience for open-ended portfolio exploration.
